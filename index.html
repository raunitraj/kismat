Beliefs

It is considered to be the centre of the Earth.

Being nearly 2000m less than that of Mt. Everest, the Kailash Parvat is still unexplorred.In fact, there is no modern-man who has ever climbed it.

It is considered to be one of the holy places not only for Hindus but also for Buddhist,Jains and Bons,

It is believed that one free from all kinds of sin can only climb the mountain.
